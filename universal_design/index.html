About Me

Hi! I’m Aja Kimsey, a third-year student at the University of Colorado Boulder pursuing a B.S. in Creative Technology and Design with a minor in Business. My focus is in UI/UX research, and I am also working toward a certificate in UX Design. I’m passionate about creating accessible, user-centered digital experiences that combine creativity with functionality. Outside of academics, I’m a Captain of the CU Boulder Club Dance Team, and I love to travel and explore new cultures around the world!
